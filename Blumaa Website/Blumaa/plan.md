

# Blumaa — Branding & Growth Agency Website

## Overview
A premium, multi-page website for Blumaa — a solo female-led branding and growth agency. The design will be editorial, conversion-focused, and confident. No fluff, all strategy.

## Color Palette
- **Blumaa Blue** (HSL: 233 100% 54%) — Primary brand color, authority, energy
- **Light Yellow** (HSL: 46 100% 83%) — Action, energy, CTAs, Secondary accent, growth, freshness
- **Pink** (HSL: 301 85% 88%) — Warm accent, optimism, highlights
- **White Chocolate** (HSL: 33 100% 97%) — Warm backgrounds, breathing room
- **Night** (HSL: 240 8% 12%) — Deep neutral for text and dark backgrounds
- **Cobalt** (#ff5757 / HSL: 0 100% 67%) — Only used in special occasions where a very specific highlight is needed
- **Green** (HSL: 70 75% 55%) — Only used in special occasions where a very specific highlight is needed

## Typography & Style
- **Headlines**: Instrument Serif — elegant, editorial serif for all headings (h1-h6)
- **Body Text**: DM Sans — clean, modern sans-serif (weights: 300-800)
- **Monospace**: Roboto Mono — for code or technical elements (weights: 400-700)
- Generous whitespace, large type, bold hierarchy
- Border radius: 0.75rem for consistent rounded corners
- Subtle animations using Framer Motion for polish and engagement

## Pages & Structure

### 1. Homepage
- **Hero Section**: Full-width, bold positioning statement ("Your brand shouldn't blend in. It should command the room."), CTA to apply/contact, subtle animated element
- **Quick intro strip**: One-liner about Blumaa's approach — strategic, not decorative
- **Services preview**: 4 cards (Branding, Rebranding, Naming, Brand & Growth Strategy) linking to the full services page
- **Why Blumaa section**: 3–4 differentiation points with icons/illustrations (e.g., "Strategy-first, always", "Built for founders who think big", "One woman. Zero fluff.")
- **Featured case study**: Highlight one project with image + results teaser, linking to full case study
- **CTA banner**: "Ready to build a brand that works as hard as you do?" → Contact page

### 2. About Page
- **Story section**: Who's behind Blumaa — founder story, philosophy, girl-boss energy with substance
- **Method/Process**: Visual step-by-step (e.g., Discover → Define → Design → Grow)
- **Values/beliefs**: What Blumaa stands for — bold statements, editorial layout
- **CTA**: Lead into services or contact

### 3. Services Page
- **Four detailed service blocks**: Branding, Rebranding, Naming, Brand & Growth Strategy
- Each includes: description, what's included, who it's for, and a CTA
- Clean, spacious layout with alternating alignment for visual rhythm

### 4. Portfolio / Case Studies
- **Grid overview page**: Project cards with image, client name, category, and one-line result
- **Individual case study pages**: Each with:
  - Client overview & challenge
  - Strategic approach / solution
  - Visual showcase (placeholder images)
  - Results & impact
  - Testimonial quote (placeholder)
  - CTA to start a project

### 5. Contact / Apply Page
- **Headline**: "Let's see if we're a fit." — confident, qualifying tone
- **Strategic application form** with fields:
  - Name & email
  - Business name & website (optional)
  - Service interest (dropdown)
  - Business stage (e.g., startup, established, pivoting)
  - Budget range (dropdown)
  - Timeline
  - "Tell me about your brand challenge" (textarea)
- **Sidebar**: FAQ-style "What to expect" or "How it works" to set expectations

### 6. Navigation & Footer
- **Header**: Clean top nav with logo, page links, and "Apply Now" CTA button in orange
- **Footer**: Logo, quick links, social media links, tagline, copyright

---

## Key Design Principles
- Every section serves a purpose — no filler
- CTAs are clear and conversion-focused throughout
- Tone is confident, strategic, and subtly witty in microcopy
- Mobile-responsive with the same editorial quality
- Placeholder content written in Blumaa's tone of voice (ready to customize)
